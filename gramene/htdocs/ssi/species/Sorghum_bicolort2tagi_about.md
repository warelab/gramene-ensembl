About *Sorghum BTx623*
---------------------
**BTx623**, a diploid reference genome for *Sorghum bicolor*, has a genome size of **719.90 Mb** and 10 haploid chromosomes. It is widely recognized as the primary reference for sorghum genetics and genomics, playing a key role in studies of C4 photosynthesis and adaptation to semi-arid conditions. The latest **telomere-to-telomere (T2T) assembly** offers a more complete and accurate version of the genome, filling in previously missing sequences and correcting assembly errors (*Wei et al., 2024*).
