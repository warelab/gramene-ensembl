### Assembly
* **DNA Extraction & Sequencing:** High molecular weight (HMW) DNA from young leaves was extracted and sequenced using **PacBio HiFi**, **Oxford Nanopore Technology (ONT)** ultra-long reads, and **Illumina short reads**. ONT and PacBio reads were assembled using **Hifiasm**, and Hi-C data was used for scaffolding.
* **Genome Assembly:** The T2T assembly resulted in a **719 Mb** genome, with all gaps closed and misassembled regions corrected. The assembly showed **high coverage** and uniform distribution across chromosomes, with an **N50 of 58.99 Mb**.
