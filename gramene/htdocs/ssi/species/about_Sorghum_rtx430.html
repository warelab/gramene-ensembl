About Sorghum bicolor RTx430

Sorghum bicolor RTx430 is a grain sorghum inbred commonly used as pollinator in hybrid production, whose genome is known to be rich in repeats.

Germplasm synonyms:

  • GRIN: PI 655996

Population Panels

S. bicolor ‘RTx430’ (GRIN: PI 655996) was derived from (Tx2536 crossed with SC0170-6-5-1-E2)-10- 4-4-1-4-X. 2 (in "E2)" is a subscript. X represents one generation of selfing, and is part of the following population panels:

  • Sorghum Association Panel (SAP) - 407 accessions (Casa et al, 2008)
