About Vinis vinifera L. ssp. vinifera Flame Seedless clone Parlier

The Flame Seedless is a table grape cultivar that keeps well in storage. It is a hybrid of Thompson Seedless, Cardinal, and several other domesticated Vitis vinifera cultivars. It produces large clusters of medium-large red grapes with a sweet flavor. Source: Wikipedia.

Assembly

As described in Zou et al (2020), high-molecular-weight DNA from Vitis vinifera cv. Flame Seedless was prepared as a 10X Genomics library and sequenced to 47-fold coverage on an Illumina NovaSeq sequencer to generate linked-reads. The linked-read data were assembled using a Supernova assembler. The total length of the assembly is 543 Mbp with a scaffold N50 of 1980 kbp.

Annotation

It is estimated that gene annotation was projected via alignment with the reference genome PN40024.
