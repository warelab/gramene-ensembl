### Assembly
* **DNA Extraction & Sequencing:** High molecular weight (HMW) DNA was extracted and sequenced using **PacBio HiFi**, **Oxford Nanopore (ONT)** ultra-long reads, **Illumina short reads**, and Hi-C data. The contigs were assembled using **Hifiasm**, and Hi-C data was used for scaffolding.
* **Genome Assembly:** The **T2T assembly** produced a **722.96 Mb** genome, with all gaps closed and assembly errors corrected. The assembly is highly contiguous and gap-free, covering centromeres and telomeres with high accuracy.
