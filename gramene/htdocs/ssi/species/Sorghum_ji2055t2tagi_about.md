About *Sorghum Ji2055 *
---------------------
**Ji2055** is an inbred line of *Sorghum bicolor* originating in China, with a genome size of **722.96 Mb**. Known for its red seeds, Ji2055 has contributed to the release of numerous commercial sorghum varieties and is recognized for its agronomic traits, including disease resistance and environmental adaptability (*Yuan et al., 2024*). This genome serves as an essential resource for studying sorghum’s genetic diversity and potential for improvement.
